# MealPilot Vision

## Vision Statement

MealPilot is an AI-powered Family Wellness and Preventive Health Copilot that helps families make better food and wellness decisions with minimal effort.

The platform combines meal planning, healthy habit tracking, activity monitoring, shopping assistance, and contextual recommendations to reduce decision fatigue around food and healthy living.

MealPilot does not aim to be a calorie counter, static recipe catalog, or simple weight tracking application.

Instead, it acts as a trusted copilot that understands family goals, preferences, habits, activities, and context to recommend the next best action.

## North Star

Reduce decision fatigue around food and healthy habits through AI.

## Long-Term Vision

Become the operating system for family wellness and preventive health.

Future capabilities include nutrition, physical activity, healthy habits, shopping assistance, sleep tracking, laboratory results, medical studies, medication management, preventive health monitoring, and professional guidance.

## Success Criteria

Users spend less time deciding what to eat, what to cook, what to buy, how to adapt meal plans, and how to stay aligned with wellness goals while improving adherence to healthy habits.
